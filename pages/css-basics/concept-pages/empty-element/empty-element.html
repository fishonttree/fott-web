Some HTML elements are self-closing. They kinda do their own thing without an argument. The easiest one is the line break, or br/.
Strangely enough, lines are capped at 80 characters, but if we want a hard line break, this is the use of it.
That being said, line break should not be stacked. That's the job of CSS.

The hr/ tag is for "horizontal rule", or a thematic break.
